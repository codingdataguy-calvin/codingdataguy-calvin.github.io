Background

What I do &
how I work

I am a data quality and governance specialist with a track record of delivering high-impact engagements in complex, data-dense environments. My work sits at the intersection of data quality, governance, and business risk — helping organisations understand the true state of their data and build the structures to keep it healthy.

I have worked across telecommunications, financial services, energy & utilities, and other regulated industries, with particular depth in subscriber data integrity, KYC compliance, master data management, and enterprise-wide data stewardship. My engagements include M&A due diligence contexts where data findings have had direct board-level commercial consequences.

I hold hands-on experience with enterprise-grade governance and quality platforms including Collibra and Informatica — tools that matter in the organisations I target. My approach is always root-cause oriented: finding the systemic reasons data problems exist, not just cleaning the symptoms.

Subscriber Data Integrity
in an M&A Context

Telecoms Data Quality M&A Due Diligence Nigeria

A full-lifecycle data quality audit for a Nigerian telecoms operator undergoing ownership transition. Spanning ~20 million subscriber records, the engagement identified 1.8M duplicate accounts, 4M inactive subscribers, and a critical credit recovery risk — directly informing the acquisition valuation.

29% reduction in reported subscriber base after deduplication and inactivity classification
600+ enterprise accounts misclassified — revenue leakage corrected across highest-value segment
Student credit scheme risk quantified — unrecoverable debt surfaced for financial auditors
Full governance framework designed, embedded, and monitored over 30 days post-delivery
